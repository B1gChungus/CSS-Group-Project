What We Do

L&L insurance supplies you with affordable, high-quality insurance for your loved ones. In the case of their death, we will supply you with money in order to afford funeral services and more.


Pricing

Regular insurance:

Male/Female Age 30-39: $350/mo

Male/Female Age 40-49: $400/mo

Male/Female Age 50-60+: $500/mo

Special Requests

Special requests are rarely accepted as the person you are insuring has to have a chronic illness or suffering from a terminal disease. In the case either of these are acceptable, we are able to compensate you an extra sum of money which is usually around $10-15 thousand.
